What Feminism is...

The definition of feminism is: The advocacy of women's rights on the basis of the equality of the sexes.

Feminism is not saying that women are better than men, it is promoting the equality between men and women.

Feminism

♀ Supporting women of every color, shape, and size.

♀ Acknowledging all genders as equals.

♀ Confronting whomever makes a comment degrading women.

♀ Being able to grow from past patriarchy and promoting a more progessive version of equality.

Not Feminism

♀ Women degrading other women based on things they can't help.

♀ Believeing women are above men.

♀ Supporting sexism against men.

♀ Fighting for a world where women hold all of sociiety's power.
